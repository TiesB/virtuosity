Symposium Virtuosity

Every year, study association Inter-Actief organizes a symposium. This year, the theme will be the wonderful world of Art, Entertainment and their connections with IT. This encapsulates subjects such as interactive environments, auditive and visual technologies and simulation and animation of imagery. The symposium will take place in or around Enschede on the 15th of March, 2017.
